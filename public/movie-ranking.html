Best Movies According to Aphrodite

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lala Land

“La La Land” serves to remind us that movies can still be magical, and they can still provide the channel for us to see magic in the world around us.

Shrek

No animated being has ever moved, breathed or had its skin crawl quite as convincingly as Shrek, and yet the movie doesn't look like a reprocessed version of the real world; it's all made up, right down to, or up to, Shrek's trumpet-shaped ears.

Barbie

Very nice movie, with interesting message.
